    ⚠️ Microsoft Edge is a good browser, it's very fast and consumes very little RAM and resources.

    It's based on Chromium, which means it's compatible with Chrome extensions. I recommend it for those who want to use Google Chrome, as it's faster and lighter.

    On the other hand, this browser is known to run in the background, collecting and sending data to Microsoft. (Just as Google does with Google Chrome, by the way).

    If you want to use it as your main browser or keep it, you can skip to the next step: Activation

    Personally, I recommend installing Firefox, it's world-renowned and open-source.

    Furthermore, it also offers extensions, an account to synchronize searches on all your devices and is available on Android, IOS, MacOS.
